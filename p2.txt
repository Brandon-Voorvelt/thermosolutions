Thermo-Solutions is a roofing insulation manufacturing company based in the beautiful city of Cape Town, Western Cape.
We are an <b> environmentally-friendly </b> company, with a <i> family type culture </i> that strives on reducing our carbon footprint and supporting <b>local businesses</b>. 
We use recycled P.E.T for our products, and get all our materials from local businesses. 

	We strive on delivering the best product - with the best service.
	We are even recommended by government enterprises, such as Eskom.
	

      We cater to the requirements of South Africa with regards to the law passed in December 2011 that requires 
      all new dwellings and structures to be fitted with roofing insulation.
